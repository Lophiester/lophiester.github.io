Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Your privacy and the safety of your pets are our top priorities. Sniff Radar was designed to be a collaborative, secure community to help lost pets find their way back home.

Data Collection and Use

To facilitate our community-based pet recovery services, we collect certain information:

Personal Information

  • Identity & Contact: When you sign up via Apple or Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used to create your account and identify you as a reporter in "Paw Alerts".
  • Contact Details: You may choose to provide additional contact info for pet recovery purposes.

Pet Information

  • Pet Profiles: We store information you provide about your pets, including their names, breeds, physical descriptions, and photos. This data is shared with the community when a "Paw Alert" is active.

Location Information

  • Precise Location: To send you real-time alerts about lost pets in your vicinity, the app requires access to your precise location.
  • Background Location (Always Allow): Sniff Radar uses Geofencing technology to notify you when you enter an area where a pet has been reported lost. This requires "Always" location access, even when the app is in the background. We only use this data to trigger these critical safety notifications.

Data Storage and Third-Party Services

We use secure industry-standard services to protect your data:

  • Firebase (Google): We utilize Firebase for authentication, secure real-time database (Firestore), and media storage (Firebase Storage).
  • Push Notifications (APNs/FCM): We use these services to send you immediate updates about pets and community messages.

Data Sharing

Your pet's information and your reporter identity are shared with other Sniff Radar users ONLY when needed to facilitate the recovery of a lost pet or through community feed interactions. We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

Security

We implement several security measures, including environment checks (Jailbreak detection), to protect the integrity of our community data. Access may be restricted on compromised devices to ensure a safe environment for all users.

Children's Privacy

Sniff Radar is intended for users who can responsibly participate in pet recovery efforts. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Effective Date" above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channel provided in the app or on our official App Store page.
